Shipping Services

ItsAmePhotography provides the below shipping services. Actual delivery time frames may vary due to lab processing time, weather, service delays, holidays, etc.

Lab processing usually takes 2 days for normal glossy prints.

Domestic shipping to Hawaii, Alaska, or Puerto Rico or any other location outside the Continental United States may take longer than expected due to their locations.

USPS First Class Mail 

  • This shipping option is available for $3.95 applying to all orders under 13oz. and has an average delivery window of 3-10 days depending on the final delivery location zip code. Tracking is available.
  • Final delivery is made by USPS.
  • Standard shipping may take a few more days for locations like Hawaii, Alaska, Puerto Rico or any orders shipped outside the United States. This service is available for APO, DPO and FPO addresses and non-contiguous U.S. states and territories.

USPS/FedEx Ground Shipping

  • Ground shipping is an expedited option for totals under $49.00 and costs $7.00
  • Ground shipping is an available shipping option throughout all 50 states.
  • Ground depends on what section of the United States the item is being shipped to
  • East Coast: 1-2 Business days
  • Midwest: 2-3 Business days
  • Southwest: 2-4 Business days
  • Northwest: 3-4 Business days
  • West Coast: 3-5 Business days
  • Alaska, Hawaii, Canada: 5-7+ Business days
  • International: 7+ Business days

Overnight Shipping

  • Overnight is an expedited shipping option that offers full door-to-door tracking.
  • Overnight is an available shipping option throughout all 50 states for $30.00
  • There is at least 2 days of processing before the product is shipped.
  • This service is not available for P.O. Boxes or APO, DPO and FPO addresses.
